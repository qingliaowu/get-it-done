# Get It Done

**Get It Done** is a comprehensive productivity and lifestyle Android application designed to help you manage tasks, track habits, and improve your well-being through gamification.

## Features

*   **To-Do List**: Manage your daily tasks with ease.
*   **Habit Tracker**: Build and maintain good habits with streak tracking.
*   **Digital Pet**: Grow your digital pet by completing tasks and habits. Feed and play with it!
*   **Calendar**: View your schedule and progress in a monthly view.
*   **Emotion Tracker**: Log your daily moods and keep a journal.
*   **Motivation**: Get daily motivational quotes.
*   **Customization**:
    *   **Dynamic Theming**: Choose between System, Light, and Dark modes.
    *   **Dashboard Config**: Customize your home screen by showing/hiding features.
*   **Data Management**:
    *   **Cloud Backup**: Backup your data to the cloud (Simulation).
    *   **Export**: Export your data to JSON.
    *   **Categories**: Create custom categories for your tasks.

## Tech Stack

*   **Language**: Kotlin
*   **UI**: Jetpack Compose (Material3)
*   **Architecture**: MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel)
*   **Dependency Injection**: Hilt
*   **Database**: Room (SQLite) with Migration support
*   **Preferences**: DataStore
*   **Async**: Coroutines & Flow

## Setup Instructions

1.  Clone this repository.
2.  Open the project in **Android Studio** (Hedgehog or newer recommended).
3.  Sync Gradle files.
4.  Run the application on an Android Emulator or Physical Device.
